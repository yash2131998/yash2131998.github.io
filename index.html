About us

We focus on creating repeat clients. We see ourselves as an extension of our client’s team. Clients can depend on us to provide a dedicated, friendly, and honest team to perform their projects.

Our Company believes that timely completion of project is the key ingredient for the success of builders and their clients and they have been successfully doing this for the last 13 years. Shree Ganesh construction has been at the forefront of innovation and technology in the earthmoving sector in Indore and Madhya Pradesh since 2008.
